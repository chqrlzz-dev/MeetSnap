What MeetSnap does

A focused tool for one job — capturing what's on screen during a meeting.

📸

Instant capture

Click the floating button or press Ctrl+Shift+S to capture the visible tab. No dialogs, no delays.

💾

Auto-download

Every screenshot is saved automatically as a timestamped PNG: google-meet-YYYY-MM-DD_HH-MM-SS.png

🎯

Meet-only activation

The floating button and all capture logic only activate on meet.google.com pages. No other sites are affected.

🖱️

Drag to reposition

Move the floating button anywhere on screen. It snaps to the nearest edge and remembers its position between sessions.

🔔

Visual and audio feedback

Optional shutter sound, screen flash, and ripple animation confirm each capture. All effects are individually toggleable.

Privacy policy

MeetSnap collects no analytics, installs no trackers, and stores nothing beyond your own preferences.

What MeetSnap does NOT do

  • Record audio or video
  • Perform background or continuous capture
  • Use analytics SDKs or third-party libraries
  • Store screenshots locally after download
  • Access tabs other than the active Google Meet tab
  • Track usage, behaviour, or identity

Manual installation

MeetSnap is not published on the Chrome Web Store. Install it as an unpacked extension in four steps.

  1. 01

    Download the source

    Clone or download the repository from GitHub.

    git clone https://github.com/chqrlzz/meetsnap.git
  2. 02

    Generate the icons

    Open icons/create-icons.html in Chrome and download all PNGs.

  3. 03

    Load the unpacked extension

    Open chrome://extensions, enable Developer mode, and load the folder.
